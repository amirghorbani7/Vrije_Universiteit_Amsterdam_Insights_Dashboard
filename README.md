# 📊 Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam Insights Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze institutional performance of Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam across education, research, impact, staffing, and financial dimensions.

This project is designed to support data-driven decision-making by providing a clear executive overview alongside detailed analytical insights.

## 📌 Key Insights

### 🎓 Education
- Total Students (latest year): ~31.5K  
- Balanced distribution between Bachelor and Master students  
- Growth in international student population in recent years  

---

### 🔬 Research
- Scientific Publications: ~8.2K (latest year)  
- Stable PhD output with recovery after temporary decline  
- Continuous funding through ERC and NWO grants  
- Open Access publications increased significantly (~40% → ~80%)  

---

### 🚀 Impact & Innovation
- Consistent patent activity across years  
- Growth in spin-offs and innovation projects  
- Strong media presence (~6–8K annual mentions)  
- Increasing engagement through demonstrator lab projects  

---

### 👥 Staff
- Total Employees: ~4.8K FTE  
- Strong academic workforce structure  
- Increasing internationalization of academic staff  
- Gradual improvement in female professor representation  

---

### 💰 Finance
- Total Revenue: ~€798M  
- Total Expenses: ~€801M  
- Slightly negative net result in the latest year  
- Government funding remains the dominant revenue source
